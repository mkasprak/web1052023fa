The Woman Who Rides Like a Man

by Tamora Pierce

Cover image from book

The Woman Who Rides Like a Man is youth fiction by Tamora Pierce. It is the third book out of four in her series about Alana the Lioness. Alana pretended to be a boy so that she could become a night. This book takes place after she is knighted and her secret has been revealed.
