About Us

At Meri's Muffins and Gluten-Free Treats, we're dedicated to crafting the finest gluten-free baked goods in Crystal Lake, IL. We take pride in offering a wide range of delectable treats. Our bakery is located in charming Crystal Lake, open from 5 am to 2 pm, Tuesday through Sunday. We provide high-quality gluten-free options for breakfast, brunch, or special occasions. Explore our mouthwatering cupcakes, freshly baked bread, custom cakes, treats, and vegan options.
