What's an Elimination Diet?

Imagine being a food detective. That's what an elimination diet is like. It involves temporarily removing specific foods from your diet to see if your symptoms get better. If they do, you've likely found a food sensitivity. It's a way to figure out what agrees with your body.

Symptoms of Food Intolerance

Food intolerance can show up in various ways, such as stomach discomfort, gas, or headaches. If you often feel unwell after eating certain foods, pay attention to these signs. They can be your body's way of saying that a particular ingredient isn't your friend.

How Baking Gluten-Free Differs

Gluten-free baking is a bit like a fun science experiment. You might need different amounts of liquids and binders like xanthan gum to get the perfect texture. Don't worry; as you practice, you'll become a gluten-free baking expert!
